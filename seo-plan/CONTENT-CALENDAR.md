## Recurring Rituals

### Monthly
- Update `lastmod` in sitemap for changed pages only (keep it honest).
- Refresh "today" data pages (weather/temperature) so they stay accurate (these decay fastest).
- Internal-linking sweep: any new post links to pillar + room + booking.
- Pull GSC "queries with impressions but low CTR" → rewrite titles/meta.

### Quarterly
- Competitor benchmark check (Birdcage, Parvada, Sonapani, Ojaswi review counts).
- Prune/merge any post with near-zero impressions after 6 months (thin-content control).
- Re-score top money-pages for conversion (CTA placement, offer clarity).

### Annual — December "De-Yearing" Ritual (critical)
~40+ titles/slugs say "(2026)". Each December:
- [ ] Update statistics, prices, dates, and "(2026)"→"(2027)" in **titles + meta + on-page H1** of the year-stamped posts.
- [ ] **Keep slugs unchanged** (don't break URLs — slugs with "2026" can stay; the visible title is what matters).
- [ ] Bump `dateModified` in Article schema + sitemap `lastmod`.
- [ ] Prioritise the high-traffic posts first (from GSC).
